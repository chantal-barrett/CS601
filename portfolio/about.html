About

When I'm not busy with work and school I spend my time doing one of the following:

Jet Skiing 🧜

I love being out on the water. There is something about the freedom of the ocean and the salty air that just makes me so happy. My dad also enjoys being out on the water and decided to purchase a jet ski in 2013 and since then jet skiing has become one of my favorite summer activities. When I’m on the jet ski you can find me jumping waves or, if it is a calm day and there are no waves to jump, you can find me alongside big boats trying to jump their wake. I have taken a couple of falls off the jet ski before so it’s always important to remember to wear your life vest!

Working Out 🏋

You can find me at the gym (almost) every morning at 6:00am, and sometimes if I’m caught up on homework, I go in the afternoon for a second time! I always enjoyed being active but would only do cardio at the gym as I was too intimidated to go into the weightlifting section. However, at the beginning of this year, my friend taught me how to lift and that has since become a passion of mine. I would have to say leg day is my favorite day and chest day is a close second.

Golf 🏌

I enjoy golfing, although you probably only want me on your team if we’re playing best ball 😀

Longboarding 🛹

I started longboarding shortly after I started snowboarding. I am not even close to being an expert at longboarding and can only travel a few feet before I get scared of falling. However, I still do enjoy it!

I also have a passion for snowboarding and love traveling so be sure to check those pages too!
