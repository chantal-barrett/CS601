SNOWBOARDING

Snowboarding

I started snowboarding a few years ago in 2017, right after I graduated college. I still take a couple of falls but I find it so much fun! I would go up to Mount Snow almost every weekend where I learned how to hit rails, butter, tripod, and more! However, once I started graduate school in 2020, I had to shift my focus to school work and now do not get to go snowboarding as often. I enjoy school though, as I have learned so much, yet my heart remains on the mountains.
